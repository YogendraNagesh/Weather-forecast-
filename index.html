ABOUT

Weather forecasting is the application of science and technology to predict the conditions of the atmosphere for a given location and time. People have attempted to predict the weather informally for millennia and formally since the 19th century. Today, forecasting relies on advanced computer models, pattern recognition, and meteorological expertise. It combines satellite imagery, radar data, and surface observations to produce forecasts. Accurate weather predictions help in agriculture, disaster planning, transportation, and everyday life.
